About Me

Hi! I'm Barry. I'm 42 and live in Seattle. I absolutely love Halloween. The picture to the left shows me and my girlfriend at a pumpkin beer festival during October. Some of my hobbies include board games, photography, and filmmaking.

I have a B.A. Degree in Graphic Design from The International Academy of Design and Technology and an A.A. in Computer Electronics from ECPI. After I graduated ECPI I got a job as a Copier Repairman. I then went on to work in printing for several years until I decided to go back to school and got my Graphic Design degree. I was hired by a merchandise company called BDA where I did Graphic Design, Photography, and Videography. Now I am a student at Code Fellows hoping to transition into a career in Software Development.
